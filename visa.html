Visa Information

All Cambodian visas for entering the Kingdom of Cambodia can be applied by mail or in person at the Consular Office of the Royal Embassy of Cambodia in Washington DC.

297 Campbell Avenue Toronto, ON M6P 3V7
Tel: (416)726-2318-edit

Instructions for Visa Issuance

Apply by MAIL:

If you are applying for Tourist or Business Visa by mail please be sure that you have at least 3 weeks before your departure date from the country. Any urgent request please contact our consular office.

Please submit the following:

  1. One completed visa application
  2. One current passport sized photo (2"x2")
  3. Original Passport with at least 6 (six) months validity (please don't send us the copy of your passport, it has to be original)
  4. Visa Fee
  5. A self-addressed prepaid return envelope* (Please see below for more information)

If apply for business visa: you will need supporting documentation such as a letterhead from your company and/or invitation from Cambodia and along with all above requirements.

(Visas will be provided free of charge for children aged under 12 years old if traveling with their parents)

Applicants Holding Canadian Passports submit the Following:

  1. One completed visa application form
  2. One current passport sized photo(2" x 2")
  3. Fee for tourist visa is 35 USD and 40 USD for business visa (Money Order from Canada Poster Service is not accepted. Please use certified's check or cashier's check from any Canadian banks or personal/company check). All payments must be payable to: Embassy of Cambodia
  4. Passport (original) with at least 6+ months validity
  5. A self-addressed prepaid return envelope.

For business visas you will need supporting documentation such as a letterhead from your company and/or invitation from Cambodia

Applicants from Afghanistan, Algeria, Bangladesh, Iran, Iraq, Pakistan, Saudi Arabia, Sri Lanka and Sudan, except for those employed by the UN, IMF,WORLD BANK, ADB and other International Organizations, must provide the following:

  1. Letter of Sponsor from his/her company, local NGO, school
  2. Return air ticket to and from Cambodia
  3. One photocopy of Alien ID Card, student ID card

A tourist visa is valid for a period of three months beginning from the issue date. It is good for a single entry for a duration of 30 days staying in the Kingdom of Cambodia. The tourist visa may be extended for a maximum period of one month.

Applicants who would like their passport returned by mail should include a prepaid envelope addressed to themselves.

*SELF-PREPAID RETURN ENVELOPE

To secure the prompt return of your passport and other documents, we reccommend that all prepaid return envelope MUST BE Certified Mail, Express Mail, UPS, Airborne or DHL (Any Type of Mail that can be easily track) and on the sender’s and receiver’s name and address must be the same (Please do not put the Royal Embassy of Cambodia as the Sender). Please contact us if you have any questions or concerns. US Postal Service is hightly recommended.

NOTE: In case the package is lost/damage during the mailing process, applicants is fully responsible for the problems that may occur.

Instruction For Follow-Up on Your Visa Status If You Send by Mail

Please be sure to check your tracking number prior calling our consular office. Then call us at (202)-997-7031. Also, you can email us at consular.camemb.usa@gmail.com. Please, allow up to 5 business days (for non-peak season June-August) and up to 10 business days (for peak season September-May) for us to complete the visa process.

Please have the following information ready before you call : First and Last name, Which Delivery Service You Sent Your Package (USPS, UPS), Type of package (Express Mail, Certified Mail, Ground/Air Mail, or Regular Stamp Mail), Package Sent Date.

Extension Visa

Your visa can be extended while you are in Cambodia. Diplomatic (A), Official (B) and Courtesy (C) visas can be extended at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation, Phnom Penh, Cambodia.

The Business (E) Visa can be extended for one month, three months, six months or twelve months ( can be single or Multiple entries ) at the Immigration Department in front of Phnom Penh Airport ).

The Tourist (T) visa can be extended for one month only at the Immigration Department in front of Phnom Penh Airport).

Diplomatic and Official Passport

Each applicant must fully complete the following:

  1. One visa application form
  2. One passport sized photograph (2 X2).
  3. Passport (valid for at least six months)
  4. Official Verbal Note which explain in detail the purpose of visiting Cambodia.

Diplomatic, Official, Courtesy and Special K visas are issued gratis. Children under the age of 12 are also issued gratis visas.

Khmer Visa (K)

Khmer Visa is issued free of charge to Cambodian Citizen living abroad. Applicants submit the following:

  1. One visa application form
  2. One passport sized photograph (2 X2).
  3. Passport (valid for at least six months)
  4. Proof of Cambodian Citizenship
  5. No fee

e-Visa: Please visit the e-Visa page for information on how to obtain a visa electronically.
